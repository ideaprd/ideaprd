1.2 Concepts

Key ideas used in this repository, mapped to files you can open locally.

Navigation vs content

  • nav.json: menu tree; the language home page fetches it and renders links.
  • pages/*.html: self-contained pages with inline CSS for easy caching and edits.

Passive discovery

robots.txt points crawlers to sitemap.xml, which lists absolute URLs under https://prd.ideaprd.com for indexing.
